Hi, I'm Tim, and I'm working on this site for fun.

Good day to start writing: Goosemas has been announced — the 13th one,
December 11 and 12, 2026 at Boardwalk Hall in Atlantic City. Head over and
[sign up here](https://events.seated.com/goosemas-2026); signing up gets you a
code for the presale on Wednesday, July 22, and the public on-sale is Friday,
July 24.

As of today, the only [[song:atlantic-city]] in the index is the song. Come
December, the city gets its first entry.

## The packages, laid out

and they go on sale first — Tuesday, July 21 at 10 a.m. ET, a day ahead of the
presale. The whole spread on one card:

Three readings worth pulling out of it:

- Super TED is the only all-or-nothing tier — both nights, $764 on the floor or
  $824 in the front two rows, plus fees. It carries the extras: the soundboard
  viewing section, the Night Zero party on Thursday, December 10, and the
  night-one family photo. TED VIP comes as two nights ($519 / $579) or one
  ($275 Friday, $305 Saturday).
- Travel packages pair a two-night hotel stay with two 2-night tickets, fees
  and taxes included, from $1,122.44 for two at the Courtyard to $3,005.64 for
  Super TED in Premium Reserved at the Ocean. Two regularities hide in the
  grid: every per-person price is exactly half the package, and Premium
  Reserved seating adds exactly $128.40 to any TED or Super TED package, at
  every hotel.
- Prices are as posted July 20 on
  [the packages page](https://goose2026.100xhospitality.com/) — that page is
  the box office; this card is a reading of it.
