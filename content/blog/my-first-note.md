## The packages, laid out

Same-day update: the
[VIP and travel packages](https://goose2026.100xhospitality.com/) are posted —
on sale Tuesday, July 21 at 10 a.m. ET, a day before the presale. Prices as
posted July 20; that page is the box office.

### VIP tickets — per person, plus fees

- **Super TED, both nights** — $764 floor / $824 front two rows. Soundboard
  viewing, Shenanigans lounge, Thursday Night Zero party, family photo, foil
  poster.
- **TED, both nights** — $519 / $579 front seven rows. TED bar, Saturday
  afterparty, paper poster.
- **TED, one night** — $275 Friday / $305 Saturday. Same perks, no afterparty.
