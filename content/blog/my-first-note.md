Hi, I'm Tim, and I'm working on this site for fun.

Good day to start writing: Goosemas has been announced — the 13th one,
December 11 and 12, 2026 at Boardwalk Hall in Atlantic City. Head over and
[sign up here](https://events.seated.com/goosemas-2026); signing up gets you a
code for the presale on Wednesday, July 22, and the public on-sale is Friday,
July 24.

As of today, the only [[song:atlantic-city]] in the index is the song. Come
December, the city gets its first entry.

## The packages, laid out

Updated later the same day: the
[VIP and travel packages](https://goose2026.100xhospitality.com/) are posted,
and they go on sale first — Tuesday, July 21 at 10 a.m. ET, a day ahead of the
presale. Everything below is as posted July 20 on
[the packages page](https://goose2026.100xhospitality.com/) — that page is the
box office; this post is a reading of it.

### The weekend

- **Thursday, December 10** — the Night Zero kickoff party. Venue and lineup
  TBA; guaranteed for Super TED guests only.
- **Friday, December 11** — Goose at Boardwalk Hall, night one.
- **Saturday, December 12** — night two, with an afterparty after the show
  (also TBA).

### VIP tickets — per person, plus fees and taxes

- **Super TED VIP, both nights** — $764 on the floor, $824 in the front two
  rows. The full-extras tier: a viewing section at the soundboard, the
  Shenanigans lounge, Night Zero, the night-one family photo, and a foil
  poster. Two nights or nothing.
- **TED VIP, both nights** — $519 floor, $579 front seven rows, with a
  dedicated bar, the Saturday afterparty, a paper poster, and early merch
  entry.
- **TED VIP, one night** — $275 Friday or $305 Saturday: the night-of perks
  without the afterparty. Floor or front seven rows, same price.

On the floor, entry order is a nightly randomized drawing — Super TED enters
before TED.

### Travel packages for two — fees and taxes included

Each pairs a two-night hotel stay (check in Friday, check out Sunday, with a
Thursday-night add-on available) with two 2-night tickets at the tier you
pick. Prices are per package, for two:

- **Ocean Casino Resort** — GA/Premium $1,668.14 · TED $2,352.94 · Super TED
  $2,877.24 · Thursday from $176.55
- **Tropicana Atlantic City** — GA/Premium $1,496.94 · TED $2,181.74 ·
  Super TED $2,706.04 · Thursday from $138.45
- **Borgata Hotel Casino & Spa** — GA/Premium $1,389.94 · TED $2,074.74 ·
  Super TED $2,599.04 · Thursday from $165.85
- **MGM Tower at Borgata** — GA/Premium $1,475.54 · TED $2,160.34 · Super TED
  $2,684.64 · Thursday from $187.25
- **Courtyard by Marriott** — GA/Premium $1,122.44, no VIP tiers · Thursday
  from $133.75

Two regularities save a longer list: every per-person price is exactly half
the package, and Premium Reserved seating adds exactly $128.40 to any TED or
Super TED package, at every hotel. (GA and Premium Reserved packages cost the
same; the TED and Super TED prices above are the floor option.)
